About me

Hey! I'm Matilda, currently based in Stockholm and studying to become a Java developer. I didn’t grow up coding in my bedroom or building websites at age 12. Nope, I came into tech with zero experience, just a big curiosity and a love for solving tricky stuff. Fast forward to today, and I’m completely hooked, constantly excited to dive into new puzzles and expand my tech skills.

As of right now, I’ve found myself especially drawn to backend development. There’s something really satisfying about making things work behind the scenes — the logic, the structure, the flow. I'm also super eager to explore cloud technologies and figure out how all those moving parts come together in real-world applications.

I try to approach both life and code with curiosity, humor, and the mindset that every “oops” is just a disguised learning moment. I'm always up for a new challenge, especially if it involves tech, teamwork, or a really good Spotify playlist.

Furthermore, when I'm not glued to my computer, you'll find me lifting weights or out on a long walk - then getting too tired and catch the bus back. I love a good board game night with family or friends (even though I lose about nine out of ten times - hey, at least I'm lucky in love). Otherwise, I'm probably scrubbing the oven, reorganizing the kitchen cabinet, which I find oddly therapeutic. When I need a break you'll find me rewatching Friends for the 11th time - always with a stash of lollies, of course.
